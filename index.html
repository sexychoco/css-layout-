Corpse Bride(2005)
★★★★☆
1H 17M
Tim Burton, Mike Johnson
Genres : Animation Cast : Johnny Depp, Helena Bonham Carter, Emily Watson

About Movie

A fantastic story about a timid groom, Victor (Johnny Depp), who is afraid of getting married. A day before the wedding, Victor keeps making mistakes in rehearsal and runs out. While practicing alone in the forest, he has a different experience of being dragged into the underworld due to a misunderstanding by a ghost bride (Helena Bonham Carter) after putting a ring on a finger bone that protrudes above the ground.

The upper world, where there are living people, is achromatic, and there is no life in people, no change in expression, and satirical aspects are revealed, such as making relationships with people and forcing them to marry others only for money. Also, unlike the upper world, the lower world, where the bodies are located, has a colorful background, and the bodies are more noisy and friendly than living people. In a way, it can be said that the reality of materialism, which is too tight to live to the point where it is better to die, is properly sarcastic.
