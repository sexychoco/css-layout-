Corpse Bride(2005)
★★★★☆
1H 17M
Tim Burton, Mike Johnson

A fantastic story about a timid groom, Victor (Johnny Depp), who is afraid of getting married. A day before the wedding, Victor keeps making mistakes in rehearsal and runs out. While practicing alone in the forest, he has a different experience of being dragged into the underworld due to a misunderstanding by a ghost bride (Helena Bonham Carter) after putting a ring on a finger bone that protrudes above the ground.
